How the Hotel Pilot Works
1

Capture

Connect email, intake form, or PMS. Chipper reads all inbound hotel inquiries — group blocks, retreat, catering, catering quotes, and event leads.

2

Score

Each inquiry gets a recovery value: lead age, booking value, event type, and current status. Hot leads surface in under 60 seconds.

3

Draft

Generate a follow-up that matches the hotel's voice and the guest's actual request. Proposal, room block, menu package — whatever fits.

4

Approve

Human operator or GM reviews and edits before anything goes out. Nothing sends automatically — you stay in control.

5

Measure

Track replies, appointments, bookings, and recovered revenue weekly. Every dollar attributed back to the inquiry source.
